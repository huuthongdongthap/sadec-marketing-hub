These require manual fixes because they need context-aware solutions:

1. **Form Input Labels** (~100 issues)
   - Inputs without associated `<label for="id">` elements
   - Inputs without `aria-label` attributes

2. **Button Accessible Text** (~50 issues)

3. **Missing OG Tags** (Recommended)
