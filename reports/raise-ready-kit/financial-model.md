| Tier | Price (USD/mo) | Target Customer | Features |

| **Starter** | $49 | Small OCOP shops, solo founders | Basic ROI tracking, 1 project, email support |

| Factor | Analysis |

| **OCOP premium** | $1,499 justified by government budget cycles |

### Sensitivity Analysis — What Could Go Wrong

| Scenario | Impact | Mitigation |
|----------|--------|------------|
| CAC increases 2x to $240 | LTV/CAC = 14x (still healthy) | Double down on organic/referrals |
| Churn increases to 8%/mo | LTV drops to $2,112 | Product improvement, CS investment |

| Cloud costs 2x higher | Margin -5% | Optimization, reserved instances |
